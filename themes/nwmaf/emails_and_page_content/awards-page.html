MINIMUM CRITERIA

Member of the Year:

  • Current NWMAF member

Award of Excellence:

  • At least 30 years martial arts experience
  • Actively teaching and training
  • Contribution to the culture of integrity of women in the martial arts
  • Demonstration of excellence in martial arts leadership

Award of Inspiration:

  • Minimum of 5 years of training
  • Currently training and/or teaching
  • NWMAF member for at least 5 years
  • Current NWMAF member

Founders Award:

  • Contribution to the spirit and vision of NWMAF over an extended period of time

Supporting materials

Please upload a letter of support from the nominating NWMAF member. To be considered, a letter must include the following:

  1. A description of how the nominator and nominee are related
  2. Which award the nominee should be considered for
  3. A statement of why the nominator believes the member should receive the award
  4. Three references of people who can speak to the member's martial arts-related leadership, instruction, and accomplishments in the community.Please include theirr name, email, telephone, preferred method of contact.
